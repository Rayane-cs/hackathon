# Scholaria Presentation Content

## Design System

### Colors
- **Primary:** #2563EB (dominant — headers, key shapes, CTA blocks)
- **Light Blue:** #60A5FA (supporting — cards, icons, subtle backgrounds)
- **Accent:** #38BDF8 (sharp highlight — stats, tags, call-outs)
- **Background:** #F8FAFC (slide backgrounds — clean, near-white)
- **Text Dark:** #0F172A (all body and title text)
- **Text Muted:** #64748B (subtitles, captions, secondary labels)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (contrast text on dark slides)
- **Dark Background:** #0F172A (Title, Section dividers, Closing slides)
- **Deep Blue:** #1E3A8A (Section divider backgrounds)

### Fonts
- **Titles:** Trebuchet MS Bold (44-54pt)
- **Body:** Calibri (14-16pt)
- **Captions:** Calibri (11-12pt, muted color)

### Visual Rules
- Dark slides for: Title, Section dividers, Closing
- Light slides for: all content slides
- Rounded rectangles (radius 0.15") for cards and badges
- Left margin: 0.6 inches
- Generous whitespace between elements
